Sustainable Development Goals

Our AI-powered disaster management system helps predict natural disasters, providing early warnings that can significantly enhance preparedness and resilience to climate-related hazards.

By predicting natural disasters early and providing timely interventions, our system helps minimize the health risks posed by disasters such as floods, wildfires, and storms, protecting communities and healthcare infrastructures.

our emergency alerts and resource management features directly contribute to reducing mortality during natural disasters by enabling timely response and efficient resource allocation.

our project encourages collaboration across sectors, bringing together technological innovations, governmental bodies, emergency response teams, and communities to work together in mitigating the impacts of natural disasters.
