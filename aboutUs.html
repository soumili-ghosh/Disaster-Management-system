About Us

We are a team of students who are deeply committed to using our skills and knowledge to tackle real-life problems. Inspired by the devastating effects of natural disasters, we came together to create a solution that can help save lives and optimize emergency response efforts.

As students, we believe in the power of creativity and a passion for problem-solving. We are working hard to develop a disaster prediction and resource management system that can truly make a difference, using the best tools available, such as AI and real-time data.

Together, we bring a combination of technical expertise, curiosity, and a commitment to learning, which allows us to take on complex challenges and develop solutions that address some of the world's most urgent needs.
